    Overview

    We present a comprehensive investigation of histone modifications related cis-elements in tissues in Arabidopsis thaliana, rice, and maize. To decipher the epigenetic regulatory code in plants, we construct a computational framework, starting with the modelling of 12 histone modifications with sequence features.

    Requirements

    Download R for your OS (Windows, Linux, Mac) : it’s that simple. And install the R packages required in our R scripts.

    Please install the lastest verion of R.
